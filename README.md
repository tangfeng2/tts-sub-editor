## Features

SubtitleAI is your all-in-one powerhouse for creating engaging, professional videos with ease. Designed for content creators, marketers, and video editors, it streamlines the entire process from editing to final export.

### What You Can Do

- **Professional Video Editing**: A robust multi-track timeline allows you to seamlessly mix video, audio, text, and effects. Manage your assets easily and organize multiple projects efficiently.
- **Smart Captions & Text**: Transform your content with dynamic, animated subtitles tailored for modern social media (TikTok, Shorts, Reels). Includes precise motion tracking to keep text locked onto moving objects.
- **AI-Powered Subtitles**: Automatically extract Subtitles, translate video content to reach global audiences, and utilize AI batch processing to handle massive workloads effortlessly.
- **Advanced Audio & Voiceovers**: Isolate vocals, utilize multi-speaker detection, and generate incredibly natural, multi-lingual text-to-speech (TTS) voiceovers. Create your own custom AI background music to perfectly match the mood of your video.
- **High-Performance Exporting**: Export your masterpieces with blazing-fast GPU acceleration. Enjoy features like Smart Sync Mode to perfectly match video speed to voiceovers, and a powerful background render queue.
